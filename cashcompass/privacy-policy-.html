CashCompass Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30 June 2026

CashCompass is a personal finance management application developed and maintained by Mahesh Jadhav. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Google Account information (name, email address and profile photo) for authentication.
  • Financial information entered by you, including income, expenses, savings, loans, EMI, premium records and notes.
  • Camera access only when you choose to capture or scan documents.
  • Microphone access only when you choose voice input features.
  • Notification permission for reminders and finance-related alerts.
  • Basic device information required for security, synchronization and troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide finance management features.
  • Synchronize your data securely across devices.
  • Generate reports, reminders and financial summaries.
  • Improve app performance and reliability.
  • Provide AI-powered financial suggestions when requested.

3. Google Sign-In

CashCompass uses Google Sign-In for secure authentication. We receive only the information provided by Google after your consent. Your Google password is never accessible to CashCompass.

4. Firebase Services

CashCompass uses Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore and Firebase Cloud Messaging (if enabled) to authenticate users and synchronize cloud data securely.

5. Camera Permission

Camera permission is requested only when you choose features requiring image capture or document scanning. The camera is never activated without your action.

6. Microphone Permission

Microphone permission is requested only when you choose voice input features. Audio is processed only after you explicitly start recording.

7. Notification Permission

Notification permission is used only to send reminders, payment alerts and finance-related notifications.

8. Biometric Authentication

If enabled by you, biometric authentication is used only to unlock the application securely. Biometric information never leaves your device and is never stored by CashCompass.

9. Artificial Intelligence (Gemini)

CashCompass may use Google's Gemini AI to provide budgeting assistance, financial insights and personalized suggestions. Only the information required to generate the requested response is processed.

10. Cloud Backup & Synchronization

If enabled, your financial information is securely synchronized with your Firebase account. Only authenticated users can access their own cloud data.

11. Data Retention

Your information is retained only as long as necessary to provide the services you use or until you request deletion, unless retention is required by applicable law.

12. Data Sharing

CashCompass does not sell, rent or trade your personal information. Information is shared only with trusted service providers required to operate the application, such as Google Firebase and Google Gemini AI when AI features are used. We do not share your personal information with advertisers.

13. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical and physical safeguards to protect your information. Although no electronic storage method is completely secure, we continuously improve our security practices.

14. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase
  • Google Sign-In
  • Google Gemini AI

15. Children's Privacy

CashCompass is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

16. Your Rights

  • Access your information.
  • Update your information.
  • Delete your information.
  • Disable cloud synchronization.
  • Request deletion of your cloud data.

17. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated cloud data through the account deletion option available within the application (when available) or by contacting dhanishatech@gmail.com. Eligible requests will be processed within a reasonable period.

18. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will always be published on this page with the revised effective date.
