CashCompass Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30 June 2026

CashCompass ("the App") is a personal finance management application developed by Dhanisha Tech. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and what choices you have regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information depending on the features you use.

  • Google account information (Name, Email Address and Profile Photo) for authentication.
  • Financial information entered by you such as income, expenses, savings, loans, EMI details and notes.
  • Camera access only when you choose to scan documents or capture images.
  • Microphone access only when you choose voice input features.
  • Notification permission for reminders and alerts.
  • Device information required for app functionality and troubleshooting.

2. Google Sign-In

CashCompass uses Google Sign-In for secure authentication. We receive only the information that Google provides after your consent. Your Google password is never accessible to CashCompass.

3. Firebase Services

The App uses Google Firebase services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (if enabled)

These services help securely store and synchronize your data across devices.

4. Camera Permission

Camera permission is requested only when you choose features that require taking photographs or scanning documents. The camera is never used without your action.

5. Microphone Permission

Microphone permission is requested only for voice input features. Audio is recorded only after you explicitly start voice input.

6. Biometric Authentication

If enabled by you, CashCompass may use your device's fingerprint or biometric authentication to securely unlock the application. Biometric information never leaves your device. The App does not store fingerprint or face data.

7. Artificial Intelligence (Gemini)

CashCompass may use Google's Gemini AI to provide financial suggestions, budget recommendations, or productivity assistance. Only the information required for the requested AI feature is processed.

8. Cloud Backup & Synchronization

If you choose cloud synchronization, your financial records are securely stored in your Firebase account. Only authenticated users can access their own data.

9. Data Sharing

CashCompass does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your information is shared only with trusted service providers required to operate the application, such as Google Firebase.

10. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable security practices to protect your information. Although no method of electronic storage is completely secure, we continuously work to protect your data.

11. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase
  • Google Sign-In
  • Google Gemini AI

These services have their own privacy policies.

12. Children's Privacy

CashCompass is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

13. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your information.
  • Edit your information.
  • Delete your information.
  • Stop using cloud synchronization at any time.

14. Account Deletion

You may delete your account and associated application data from within the application or by contacting us.

15. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
