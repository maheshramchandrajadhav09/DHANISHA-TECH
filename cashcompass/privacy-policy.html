CashCompass Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30 June 2026

CashCompass is a personal finance management application developed by Dhanisha Tech. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, CashCompass may collect:

  • Google Account information (Name, Email Address and Profile Photo) for authentication.
  • Financial information entered by you including income, expenses, savings, loans, EMI and premium records.
  • Notes, descriptions and transaction details entered by you.
  • Camera access when you choose to capture or scan documents.
  • Microphone access when you choose voice input features.
  • Notification permission for reminders and alerts.
  • Basic device information required for app functionality, security and troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Provide personal finance management features.
  • Synchronize your data securely across devices.
  • Generate reports, reminders and financial summaries.
  • Improve app performance and reliability.
  • Provide AI-powered financial suggestions when requested.

3. Google Sign-In

CashCompass uses Google Sign-In for secure authentication. We receive only the information provided by Google after your consent. Your Google password is never accessible to CashCompass.

4. Firebase Services

CashCompass uses Google Firebase services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (if enabled)

These services securely authenticate users and synchronize cloud data.

5. Camera Permission

Camera permission is requested only when you choose features that require taking photographs or scanning documents. The camera is never activated without your action.

6. Microphone Permission

Microphone permission is requested only when you use voice input features. Audio is processed only after you explicitly start recording.

7. Notification Permission

CashCompass uses notification permission only to send reminders, payment alerts and other finance-related notifications.

8. Biometric Authentication

If enabled by you, CashCompass may use your device's fingerprint or biometric authentication to unlock the application securely. Biometric information never leaves your device and is never stored by CashCompass.

9. Artificial Intelligence (Gemini)

CashCompass may use Google's Gemini AI to provide budgeting assistance, financial insights and personalized suggestions. Only the information necessary to generate the requested response is processed.

10. Cloud Backup & Synchronization

If you enable cloud backup, your financial information is securely synchronized with your Firebase account. Only authenticated users can access their own cloud data.

11. Data Retention

Your information is retained only as long as necessary to provide the services you use. If you delete your account or request deletion, your cloud data will be removed within a reasonable period, unless retention is required by law.

12. Data Sharing

CashCompass does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Information is shared only with trusted service providers required to operate the application, such as Google Firebase.

13. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical and physical safeguards to protect your information. Although no electronic storage method is completely secure, we continuously improve our security practices.

14. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase
  • Google Sign-In
  • Google Gemini AI

These third-party services have their own privacy policies.

15. Children's Privacy

CashCompass is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

16. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your information.
  • Update your information.
  • Delete your information.
  • Disable cloud synchronization.
  • Request deletion of your cloud data.

17. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated cloud data through the account deletion option available within the application (if enabled) or by contacting us at dhanishatech@gmail.com. We will process eligible requests within a reasonable period.

18. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
